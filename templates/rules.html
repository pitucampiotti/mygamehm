Rules:

1. Each one has to write what they think the majority will answer

2.The group with the most gets 1 point. If there is a tie between 2 or more answers or everyone gave the same answer, no one gets a point

3. Whoever has written something different from the others, gets the piggy bank

4.If there are 2 or more people who wrote something different none gets the pig

5. First to reach 8 points wins and whoever has the pig at that time gets a punishment
